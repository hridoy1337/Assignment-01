FAQ

Still Have Questions?

Everything you need to know before joining 4,000+ developers, founders, and builders at DevConf 2026.

Who should attend DevConf 2026?

Whether you're a frontend developer, backend engineer, AI researcher, founder, student, or DevOps specialist, DevConf is designed for anyone passionate about building modern software.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes. Recording access depends on your ticket package. Pro and Team attendees receive lifetime access while Standard attendees receive 30-day access.

Can I transfer my ticket?

Absolutely. Tickets can be transferred to another attendee until seven days before the event.

Are meals included?

Lunch, coffee, refreshments, and networking breaks are included throughout all three conference days.

Where is the conference located?

The exact venue, accommodation partners, and travel guide will be shared with all registered attendees well before the event.

Need a custom team package?

Companies looking to register larger groups can contact our team for discounted enterprise pricing.
